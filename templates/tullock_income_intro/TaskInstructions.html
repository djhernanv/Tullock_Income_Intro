During the experiment, you will be asked to count letters. You have to count how many times the letter “a“ appears in a sequence of characters.

On the next screen you will have the opportunity to try the task five times. You will face sequences of various lengths to get a good impression of the task. To submit an answer, hit the ⏎ Enter key. If your answer is correct, the next sequence is displayed. Otherwise, the same sequence is displayed again and you can submit another answer. The timer in blue above the input field will tell you how long you take for each sequence.

Below this panel you will find an annotated image with all the elements in the screen relevant to the task. Study them well before proceeding.

Tasks solved in the next screen will not count toward your payment.

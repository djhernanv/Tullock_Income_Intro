During the experiment, you will be asked to count letters. You have to count how many times the letter “a“ appears in a sequence of characters.

On the next screen you will have the opportunity to try the task five times. You will face sequences of various lengths to get a good impression of the task. To submit an answer, hit the ⏎ Enter key. If your answer is correct, the next sequence is displayed. Otherwise, the same sequence is displayed again and you can submit another answer. The timer in blue above the sequence will tell you how long you take for each sequence.

An annotated tour in the next screen will show you the positioning of all the relevant elements (Timer, Sequence and Input Field) along with a short explanation.

Tasks solved in the next screen will not count toward your payment.
